A brightly colored, futuristic digital literacy workshop template with five pillars and icons.

Pillar 2: "NAVIGATING THE ONLINE WORLD," green with a magnifying glass and bullet points on browsers, evaluation, fact-checking, and a challenge.
Pillar 3: "COMMUNICATION & COLLABORATION," yellow with an email and speech bubbles and bullet points on email etiquette, video conferencing, cloud tools, and sending emails.
Pillar 4: "SAFETY & SECURITY," orange with a shield and locks and bullet points on passwords, phishing, updates, and spotting phish.
Pillar 5: "DIGITAL FOOTPRINT & REPUTATION," purple with an avatar and network lines and bullet points on understanding footprints, netiquette, reputation, and Google analysis.

The pillars are connected by blue tubes with orange lights, and the header reads "DIGITAL LITERACY WORKSHOP TEMPLATE" and "CORE SKILLS & LEARNING PATHWAY."
